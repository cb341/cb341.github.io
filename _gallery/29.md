Node based terrain generation sketch created in Blender to iterate quickly on procedural composition.

Multiple noise fields are layered, remapped, and thresholded to explore different structural features. By adjusting frequency, amplitude, and cutoff parameters, the system produces caves, ore distributions, floating terrain fragments, and large scale landmasses.

Using nodes allowed rapid experimentation with generation rules before implementing similar ideas programmatically. A rough prototype of terrain logic rather than a rendering setup.
