Implementation of greedy meshing for voxel terrain. Coplanar faces of identical material are merged along the x axis and subsequently along the y axis to form maximal rectangles.

This reduces triangle count and vertex buffer size significantly, lowering GPU upload bandwidth and draw overhead in Three.js. The tradeoff is increased CPU mesh construction cost and incompatibility with transparent geometry where strict ordering must be preserved.
